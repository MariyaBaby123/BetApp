Rules

  • Betting for a match will stop an hour before the match
  • If a participant who has entered the game is unable to make a prediction for a match it will be treated as loss.
  • World cup final rules
    • There are 6 options : ( match bounty is 100 per person )
      • CROATIA WINS ( minimal bounty )
      • CROATIA WINS IN EXTRA TIME ( 2 times minimal bounty )
      • CROATIA WINS IN PENALTY ( 2.5 times minimal bounty )
      • FRANCE WINS ( minimal bounty )
      • FRANCE WINS IN EXTRA TIME ( 2 times minimal bounty )
      • FRANCE WINS IN PENALTY ( 2.5 tims minimal bounty )
    • The idea is that the winning money ( sum of all losers money which is 100 per person ) will be divided based on the weights for eg , if france wins in extra time , then whoever voted for option 4 or 5 will be winners. But option 5 winners will get 2 times more money than option 4 winners. The idea is that , people voting for specific options should get more money if they win because they are taking more risk . If 12 people votes out of which only 2 people won ( assuming france won in extra time ). among those 2 , if 1 voted for "FRANCE WINS" and 1 voted for "FRANCE WINS IN EXTRA TIME". so losing money is 1000 ( 10 people who lost * 100 per match ) which will be divided in ( 1:2 ratio ) , so the one voted for "FRANCE WINS" will get 333.33 and one voted for "FRANCE WINS in EXTRA TIME" will get 666.66
  • Knock out stage rules considering the France vs Argentina match as reference
    • If France or Argentina are tied at the end of regular time (90) and extra time(30 minutes), match advances into a penalty shoot-out
    • If the result of the penalty shoot-out is a France win or a Argentina win, only those who selected option 3 will win the bounty.
    • Option 1 and 2 will be considered as a failed selection regardless if the team they selected won the penalty shoot-out or not
